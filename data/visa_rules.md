# Visa Rules – Quick Reference

This document provides visa and entry rules for common travel routes. Always verify with the official embassy or government website before travel.

---

## Japan

### UAE passport holders

UAE passport holders can enter Japan visa-free for up to 30 days for tourism. Passport must be valid for at least 6 months.

### USA, UK, and EU/Schengen passport holders

Citizens of the USA, UK, and EU/Schengen countries can enter Japan visa-free for short-term stays of up to 90 days for tourism or business. Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from the date of entry.

### Other nationalities

For stays longer than the visa-free period, or if your nationality is not listed above, a visa must be obtained in advance from a Japanese embassy or consulate. Transit visas may be required for some connections.

---

## France and Schengen Area

### Visa-free entry (short stay)

Nationals of the UAE, USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and many other countries can enter France and the Schengen area visa-free for up to 90 days within any 180-day period for tourism or business. Passport must be valid for at least three months beyond the intended date of departure from the Schengen area.

### Schengen countries

The Schengen area includes France, Germany, Italy, Spain, the Netherlands, Belgium, Austria, Portugal, Greece, and several other European countries. One visa or visa-free entry applies to the whole zone within the 90/180-day rule.

---

## United Kingdom

### UAE passport holders

UAE passport holders can visit the UK as standard visitors for up to 6 months without a visa for tourism, business, or short-term study. Passport must be valid for the full duration of stay.

### USA, EU, and other nationals

USA and EU citizens, among others, can typically enter the UK for up to 6 months as visitors without a visa. Passport validity requirements apply; check official UK government guidance for your nationality.

---

## United Arab Emirates (UAE)

### Visa on arrival and visa-free

Many nationalities receive a visa on arrival or visa-free entry to the UAE (e.g. 30 or 90 days) for tourism. Passport validity of at least 6 months is usually required. Citizens of GCC countries typically do not need a visa for the UAE.

---

## Thailand

### Visa-free and visa on arrival

Many nationalities, including UAE, USA, UK, and most EU countries, can enter Thailand visa-free for 30 days (by air) or obtain a visa on arrival for 15–30 days depending on nationality. Passport must be valid for at least 6 months. Extension of stay is possible from within Thailand at immigration offices.

---

## Turkey

### Visa and e-Visa

Nationals of the UAE and many other countries can obtain an e-Visa online before travel for short stays (e.g. 90 days within 180 days for tourism). Some nationalities are visa-exempt for short stays. Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from entry. Check the official Turkish e-Visa website for your nationality.

---

## Singapore

### Visa-free entry

Citizens of the UAE, USA, UK, EU, Australia, and many other countries can enter Singapore visa-free for short stays (typically 30 or 90 days). Passport must be valid for at least 6 months. Proof of onward travel may be requested.

---

## General rules

- **Passport validity**: Most countries require at least 3–6 months validity beyond your stay. Always check the destination country’s rules.
- **Onward travel**: Some countries require proof of onward or return travel and sufficient funds.
- **Transit**: Transit visas may be required if you leave the airport or if your nationality is not exempt. Check the transit country’s rules.
- **Updates**: Visa rules change frequently. Always confirm with the official embassy or government website of the destination country before booking or travel.
