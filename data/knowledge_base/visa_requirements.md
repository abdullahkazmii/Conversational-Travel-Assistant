# Visa Requirements by Country

## Japan

UAE passport holders can enter Japan visa-free for up to 30 days for tourism. Passport must be valid for at least 6 months.

Citizens of many other countries (e.g. USA, UK, EU Schengen) can also enter Japan visa-free for short-term stays of 90 days or less for tourism or business. Passport validity of at least 6 months is required.

For stays longer than the visa-free period, a visa must be obtained in advance from a Japanese embassy or consulate.

## France and Schengen Area

Nationals of UAE, USA, UK, and many other countries can enter France and the Schengen area visa-free for up to 90 days within any 180-day period for tourism or business. Passport must be valid for at least three months beyond the intended date of departure from the Schengen area.

## United Kingdom

UAE passport holders can visit the UK as standard visitors for up to 6 months without a visa for tourism, business, or study (short courses). Passport must be valid for the full duration of stay.

## General Notes

- Visa rules change; always check the official embassy or government website of the destination country before travel.
- Some countries require proof of onward travel or sufficient funds.
- Transit visas may be required for certain connections even when not leaving the airport.
